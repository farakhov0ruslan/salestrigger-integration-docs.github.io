What is SalesTrigger?

SalesTrigger helps your team win more deals by detecting buyer-intent signals and trigger events across the web, then telling you who to contact right now. Connect it to your stack via n8n, Make, or call the REST API directly.

Under the hood, SalesTrigger powers intent-based prospecting with automated data collection and enrichment, and can surface people actively looking for your product by tracking the professional events they attend.
